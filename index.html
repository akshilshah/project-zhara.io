02 · Zhara Search

Search that understands intent.

Replace keyword matching with true intent understanding.

Traditional site search matches keywords. Zhara Search understands what buyers actually mean — weight, use case, price sensitivity — then recommends the right products.

  • Replaces your existing site search with an AI-powered layer.
  • Indexes full product metadata — specs, reviews, descriptions, tags, inventory.
  • Understands natural language, not just keywords.
  • Returns ranked recommendations based on what buyers mean, not what they typed.
